Deployment

Deployment models for your data-control needs.

Zuraio is designed to support deployment and storage choices that reflect company data-control needs. The right model depends on the organisation, use case, sensitivity of data and agreed technical setup.

Three models

Local, hybrid and Swiss-hosted options.

These descriptions are illustrative. Specific capabilities, pricing and availability must be confirmed before customer communication.

Local

On-premises or private cloud within your infrastructure.

  • Maximum control over where data is processed and stored
  • Suitable for organisations with strict residency or air-gap requirements
  • May require more internal IT involvement for setup and maintenance
  • Model routing can include local or approved cloud models depending on policy

Hybrid

Mix of company-controlled storage and managed AI Hub services.

  • Company knowledge may remain in approved internal systems
  • AI orchestration and selected processing in agreed locations
  • Flexible model routing between local and cloud options by task sensitivity
  • Often a practical starting point for mid-sized organisations

Swiss-hosted

Managed deployment with data residency in Switzerland.

  • Designed for Swiss companies that want managed infrastructure with Swiss data residency
  • Reduced internal infrastructure burden compared to fully local deployment
  • Permissions, audit and model routing still configurable per organisation
  • Subject to agreed service terms and verified hosting details
