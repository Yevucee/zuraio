Answers to common questions about Zuraio AI Hub, data control and getting started. Where details are not yet confirmed, this is noted explicitly.

Zuraio AI Hub is designed as a controlled AI layer for companies — connecting approved internal knowledge and business systems to practical AI workflows for meetings, email, reports, search and follow-up. It is intended for companies that want AI benefits while keeping control of data, access and model choice.

Zuraio is designed for Swiss companies and mid-sized organisations that need control over data, access, AI model choice and internal knowledge — including owner-managed businesses, companies with sensitive commercial information and teams that want practical AI rather than abstract experiments.
