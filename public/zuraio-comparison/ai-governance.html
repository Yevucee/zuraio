AI governance

Governance for responsible AI use in your company.

Zuraio is designed to help companies use AI with clearer rules around access, sources, model choice and review — preparing for internal governance and future audit expectations.

Transparency

Know what the system used and why.

Trust in AI-assisted work depends on being able to understand how outputs were produced. Zuraio is designed with transparency in mind — though the exact capabilities depend on your deployment and configuration.

Source visibility

Where internal knowledge is used, users may be able to see which documents or systems contributed to an answer. This is intended to support verification before acting on AI-generated content.

Model routing visibility

Organisations may be able to define and review which models handle which types of work, depending on sensitivity and policy.

Clear boundaries

Zuraio is designed to operate within defined permissions and approved sources — not as an unrestricted gateway to all company data or external models.

Human review

Outputs are intended to be reviewed by the people responsible for the work. AI assists; humans decide what is sent, shared or acted on.

Oversight

Oversight built into the workflow.

AI governance is not a separate compliance exercise — it should be part of how work gets done. Zuraio is designed to support oversight at several levels.

Administrative controls

  • Define which sources are connected
  • Configure user and role permissions
  • Set model routing policies by task type
  • Manage deployment and access boundaries

Activity review

  • Log requests and model usage according to agreed audit model
  • Review which sources were accessed
  • Support internal audit and governance processes

Gradual rollout

  • Start with selected use cases and users
  • Pilot before company-wide deployment
  • Review results and refine policies
  • Expand based on proven value and governance readiness
Regulatory context

Preparing for evolving expectations.

AI governance requirements are evolving in Switzerland and the EU. Zuraio is designed to help companies prepare for internal review and future audit expectations — but we do not claim compliance with any specific regulation unless verified.
