Augmenting people

AI that supports your people — not replaces them.

Zuraio is designed around a simple principle: AI should support human judgement, not replace it. The goal is to reduce repeated preparation, surface relevant context and help teams work faster — while keeping people in control of decisions, approvals and final outputs.

People stay accountable

Outputs are designed to be reviewed, edited and approved by the people responsible for the work. Zuraio assists with drafts and summaries; your team decides what is sent, shared or acted on.

Sources you can check

Where internal knowledge is used, answers can include references to the documents or systems they came from. This is intended to make it easier to verify information before acting on it.

Workflows that fit how you work

Rather than asking employees to change how they work entirely, Zuraio is designed to connect to existing tools and knowledge — meeting preparation, email, reports and search in the places teams already spend their time.
