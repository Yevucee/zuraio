1. Add a stronger hero image

Please add a high-quality hero image on the homepage.

The image should feel like a real Swiss business environment, not a generic AI stock image.

Preferred hero image direction:

* calm owner-manager or leadership team in a meeting
* middle-aged or older business decision-maker included
* documents, laptop, notebook or printed reports visible
* natural daylight
* professional but not corporate stock
* quiet, serious, trustworthy
* Swiss SME / mid-sized company feel
* no robots, no glowing AI brain, no futuristic holograms, no cyber aesthetic

Add a subtle Zuraio overlay on the image, using the brand visual language:

* “Meeting brief prepared”
* “Sources: Email · Documents · CRM”
* “Access: approved”
* “Data route: controlled”

The overlay should be minimal, elegant and not distract from the human trust of the image.

2. Reduce homepage wording

Please make the homepage more concise. Avoid long paragraphs. Each section should have:

* one clear headline
* one short paragraph, maximum 1–2 sentences
* 3–4 cards or bullets where needed
* no dense blocks of text
* clear CTAs

The homepage should feel like a calm introduction, not a full product brochure.
