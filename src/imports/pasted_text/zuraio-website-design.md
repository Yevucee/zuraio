Create a complete website design system and full multi-page website for Zuraio AI Hub.

The goal is to design a light, calm, Swiss-aware technology website for Zuraio AI Hub, aimed at Swiss companies that want practical AI for everyday work without losing control of company data.

The site must feel trustworthy to conservative Swiss business owners, owner-managers and mid-sized organisations, while still looking like a modern technology company. It should not look like a generic AI startup or hyperscaler website.

The design must include the full website, all main pages, all content sections, notes on why each section exists, and clear use of the approved brand colours, typography and visual style.

--------------------------------------------
TYPOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------

Use IBM Plex as the approved typography system.

IBM Plex Sans:
Primary typeface for headings, body copy, navigation, buttons, website UI, pitch deck references and general content.

IBM Plex Serif:
Restrained editorial accent for selected hero lines, brand promise moments, quotes and major section openers. Use sparingly.

IBM Plex Mono:
Technical/audit typeface for diagram labels, metadata, page notes, audit trails, system references, section annotations and small technical details.

Logo typeface:
Beirut remains logo-only. Do not use Beirut elsewhere.

Typography style:
- Clear hierarchy
- Large but calm hero headings
- Sentence case for headlines
- Avoid all-caps except for small mono labels
- Use one italic word sparingly in hero or promise moments if useful
- Body copy should be easy to read for older decision-makers
- Avoid tiny text in important areas
